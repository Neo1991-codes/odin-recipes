Pizza

Description

This is a home made pizza from Giyani Limpopo a small town in south africa filled with rich culture and bright colors

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough:
    • 2 quater teaspoons(1 packet) of active dry yeast
    • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
    • 3 quater cup warm water(110 degrees Fahrenheit/43 degrees celsius)
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Pizza Sauce:
    • 1 can(14-16 ounces) of crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
    • 1-2 cloves of garlic,minced
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Toppings:
    • Shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Sliced pepperoni,cooked sausage,or other preferred meats
    • Sliced bell peppers
    • Sliced onions
    • Sliced mushrooms
    • Sliced black olives
    • Sliced tomatoes
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Optional Extras:
    • Crushed red pepper flakes
    • Olive oil for drizzling
    • Fresh arugula or spinach for topping

Steps for making Pizza

  • Pizza Dough:
    1. Activate the yeast:
      • in a small bowl,combine active dry yeast,sugar, and warm water.
      • Stir and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    2. Mix the Dough:
      • in a large mixing bowl,combine all-purpose flour and salt.
      • Pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
      • Stir until the dough comes together
    3. Knead the Dough:
      • Transfer the dough to a floured surface
      • Knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
    4. Rise the Dough:
      • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl.
      • Cover it with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise for 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.
  • Pizza Sauce:
    1. Prepare the Sauce:
      • In a saucepan, heat a small amount of olive oil.
      • Add minced garlic and saute for a minute until fragrant.
      • Add crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce,dried oregano,salt,and black pepper.
      • Simmer for about 15-20 minutes to let the flavors meld
  • Assembling and Baking the Pizza:
    1. Preheat the Oven:
      • preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 475 degrees fahrenheit/ 245 degrees celsius) with a pizza stone or a baking sheet inside
    2. Roll Out the Dough:
      • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    3. Add the sauce and Toppings:
      • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or another floured surface
      • Spread the prepared pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges
      • Add a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese and your preferred toppings
    4. Bake the Pizza:
      • Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven
      • Bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned
    5. Finish and Serve:
      • Remove the pizza from the oven.
      • Optionally,drizzle olive oil and sprinkle crushed red pepper flakes
      • Top with fresh basil leaves,grated parmesan cheese, or fresh arugula or spinach, if desired.
