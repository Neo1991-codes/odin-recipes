Spaghetti

Description

This is the best pasta(spaghetti) in Limpopo

Ingredients

  • 225 grams of spaghetti pasta
  • crushed tomatoes
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic,minced
  • 1 small onion,finely chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • fresh basil leaves or dried basil
  • Fresh parsley,chopped
  • Red pepper flakes(optional for added heat)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (for garnish)
  • Ground beef or ground pork (optional for meat sauce)
  • Meatballs or italian sausage (as alternative meat options)
  • Sliced black or green olives(optional)
  • Sliced mushrooms(optional)

Steps:for making Spaghetti

  1. Boil the Spaghetti:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
    • Add the spaghetti pasta and cook according to the package instructions until it's all done (usually 8-12 minutes)
    • Drain the pasta and set it aside
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a saucepan,heat olive oil over medium heat
    • Add minced garlic and chopped onions
    • Saute until the onions become translucent
  3. Add the meat(optional):
    • If you're making a meat sauce,add ground beef or ground pork to the pan
    • Cook until the meat is browned and cooked through
  4. Season the sauce:
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
    • Add dried oregano,salt and black pepper to taste
    • Optionally,add red pepper flakes for some heat
  5. Simmer and Cook:
    • Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes,allowing the flavors to meld
  6. Add optional ingredients:
    • If desired,add sliced olives,mushrooms,bell peppers,or grated carrots
    • Simmer for an additional few minutes to cook and incorporate these ingredients
  7. Combine the pasta and sauce:
    • Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce
    • Toss everything together to coat the pasta with the sauce
  8. Garnish and Serve
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves,chopped parsley,and grated parmesan cheese
    • Serve hot and enjoy your homemade spaghetti
